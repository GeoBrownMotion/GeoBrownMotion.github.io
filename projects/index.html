TandemTools

First-author of Evaluating Rapport in Tandem Language Exchanges with Transformers Architecture in EACL 2024.

Developed transformer-based LLM models for sentiment analysis, optimizing model performance through comparative evaluations of multiple BERT-variants models, where English and Spanish participants relayed image descriptions, facilitating sentiment categorization.

Utilized MongoDB, PyTorch, and AWSEC2 to support large scale data storage, model development and training.

Architected an end-to-end pipeline, culminating in the enhancement of TandemTools capabilities.

Logarithmic Lenses

Demonstrated that using linear RGB or log RGB improves the performance of a deep network on a detection task.

Detected that the linear and log RGB networks are more robust to intensity and color balance variation.

Co-author of Logarithmic Lenses: Exploring Log RGB Data for Image Classification in CVPR 2024.
