2. What decisions did you make when you made your site mobile friendly?
- Answer: 

    I used flexible grid layouts and media queries to adjust the layout dynamically, ensuring that content was easily readable and accessible on a wide range of devices. Also I scaled down images, increasing font sizes for readability on small screens, and restructuring navigation to be more touch-friendly. In addition, I increased the size and space of interactive elements, like buttons and links to prevent accidental clicks, enhancing the overall user experience on mobile devices.
